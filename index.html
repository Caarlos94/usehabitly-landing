Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before you start building habits.

Is Habitly free to use?

Yes. Habitly is free to download and all core features — habit tracking, streaks, freeze days, XP, levels, achievements, heatmaps, and data export — are included at no cost. An optional Premium plan unlocks extras like custom motivational phrases.

Do I need to create an account to start?

No. You can start instantly with Guest Mode using only a username — no email or password required. Guest accounts are limited to 5 active habits, and you can migrate to a full account (email or Google) at any time without losing your progress.

What are Freeze Days and how do they work?

Freeze Days protect your streak on days when you can't complete a habit. You start with 2 freeze days and earn 3 extra every 5 levels. When used, a freeze day preserves your streak as if the day had been completed — so one rough day doesn't erase weeks of progress.

Can I export my habit data?

Yes. From Settings → Export Data you can export your complete habit history as CSV or PDF, with filters for last 30 days, 90 days, last year, or all time. The file is saved locally on your device and you choose whether to share it.

Is Habitly available on iOS?

Not yet. Habitly is currently available only on Android through Google Play. We're focused on polishing the Android experience before expanding to other platforms.

What languages does Habitly support?

Habitly is fully bilingual in English and Spanish, including the habit catalog, achievements, motivational phrases, and every UI string. You can switch languages anytime from settings.

Does Habitly have social features like friends or challenges?

No. Habitly is an individual experience focused on personal habit building. There are no friends, group challenges, or public leaderboards — your progress is completely private.

How do I delete my account and data?

Go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account. This permanently removes your user profile, habits, logs, XP, level, achievements, freeze days, and onboarding preferences from our servers. See our Privacy Policy for details.
