6.1 Experience Points (XP)

  • XP is awarded for completing habits and achievements
  • XP determines your level within the Application

6.2 Freeze Days

  • Freeze Days protect your streak when you cannot complete habits

  • Premium users additionally receive 5 freeze days automatically every 30 days while their subscription is active
